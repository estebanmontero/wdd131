Academy Music - Site Plan Document

Site Name

Academy Music

Reason for Selection: The name "Academy Music" clearly identifies the site as a central resource for music education, aimed at both students and teachers. It conveys professionalism and inclusivity, making it attractive to a wide audience interested in music learning and resources.

Domain : musicacademy.com.co

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Music Academy website is to provide a comprehensive resource for music students. The site will offer instructional materials, course listings, event calendars, and a platform for community engagement. Additionally, it will include information about the academy's programs, faculty, and enrollment procedures.

Scenarios

Prospective Student Scenario

Question: What music courses are available for beginners?

Answer: The site will provide a detailed list of music courses, including descriptions, schedules, and enrollment options.

Current Student Scenario

Question: How can I find the event calendar and register for upcoming music workshops?

Answer: The site will feature an easily accessible event calendar with links to registration forms for workshops and other events.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Deep Blue (#003366) - Headings, navigation bar, and footer background.
  • Secondary Color: Light Gray (#F0F0F0) - Background for content sections, sidebars, and forms.
  • Accent Color: Gold (#FFD700) - Buttons, links, and highlights.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Open Sans - Body text and paragraphs. This font is clean and easy to read, making it suitable for large blocks of text.
  • Secondary Font: Merriweather - Headings and subheadings. This serif font adds a touch of elegance and formality to the site.
  • Special Font: Roboto - Buttons and navigation links. This font is modern and bold, making it ideal for interactive elements.

Wireframe

Home Page Layout (Desktop View)

Header: Contains the site logo, navigation menu (Home, Courses, Events, Faculty, Contact).
Hero Section: A large banner image with a welcome message and a call-to-action button (e.g., "Explore Courses").
Main Content Area:

  • Section 1: Introduction to the academy with a brief overview and image.
  • Section 2: Featured courses with images and links to course pages.
  • Section 3: Upcoming events with dates and links to event details.
  • Section 4: Testimonials from students and faculty.
Sidebar: Quick links to popular content, recent blog posts, and social media feeds.
Footer: Contact information, social media links, and newsletter signup form.

Mobile View Layout

Header: Simplified logo and hamburger menu for navigation.
Hero Section: Scaled-down banner image with a welcome message.
Main Content Area:

  • Collapsible Sections for introduction, featured courses, and events.
  • Testimonials displayed as a carousel.
Footer: Stacked contact information and social media links.
