COLOMBIA MEDELLIN

Colombia is a biodiverse and multicultural country. Its landscapes, its people and its culture are such particular attributes in each city or department that sometimes, when moving from one region to another, it seems as if a change of country had occurred. In Colombia you have the opportunity to live many different experiences in the same country since it has different thermal floors. Through its center run the towering, snow-covered volcanoes and mountains of the Andes. Tropical beaches line the north and west. And there are deserts in the north and vast grasslands, called Los Llanos, in the east..
